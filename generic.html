Business License Application Process

All the application which is process licensing is in here

Log in/Registered

Users must create an account or log in to the e-Licensing platform using their valid credentials. This helps secure user data and allows the system to track and manage individual license applications

Fill in Application Form

Once logged in, applicants are required to fill out an online form with complete business information such as business name, type of business activity, premise address, and contact details. Choosing the correct license category ensures proper processing.

Upload Required Documents

Applicants must upload supporting documents such as a copy of the identity card (IC), Companies Commission of Malaysia (SSM) registration certificate, photos of the business premises, and other relevant files depending on the type of business.

Application

All the applications about licensing

1.Register / Log In

Users must create an account or log in to the e-Licensing platform using their valid credentials. This helps secure user data and allows the system to track and manage individual license applications.

2.Fill in Application Form

Once logged in, applicants are required to fill out an online form with complete business information such as business name, type of business activity, premise address, and contact details. Choosing the correct license category ensures proper processing.

3.Upload Required Documents

Applicants must upload supporting documents such as a copy of the identity card (IC), Companies Commission of Malaysia (SSM) registration certificate, photos of the business premises, and other relevant files depending on the type of business.

4.Submit Application

After completing the form and uploading all documents, applicants must review the information for accuracy and click the submit button. This sends the application to the licensing department for further action.

5.Application Review

The Majlis Daerah Tangkak (MDT) will review the application and verify all submitted documents. In some cases, officers may conduct a site inspection to ensure the business premises comply with safety and zoning regulations before approval.

6.Make Payment

Once the application is approved, applicants will receive a notification to make payment. Payment can be done online through the systemâ€™s integrated payment gateway, making the process faster and more convenient.

7.Download License

After successful payment, the applicant can download and print the official business license from their account. This license serves as legal proof of permission to operate and must be displayed at the business premises.
